School Leadership Team

The academics and operational management of Crimson Anisha Global School (CAGS) will be led by Crimson.

Crimson is a collaborative reunion of India’s leading educationalists, who have wealth of experience in developing great schools (national and international curricula) across India and the Middle East.

The team includes educators who have worked as Teachers, Principals, Operational heads, Board representatives/inspectors and Teacher education experts. Experience with a myriad of boards (both national and international) equips the Crimson team with a strong understanding to transform the CAG schools to match the aspirations of the National Education Policy 2020, recently released by the Government of India.
